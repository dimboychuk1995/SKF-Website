About SKF Trucking

SKF Trucking is a veteran-friendly auto transport company built around direct communication, dependable scheduling, and safe nationwide delivery.

We focus on car shipping across the continental United States with routes that frequently connect Detroit to major Midwest markets while still supporting long-distance coast-to-coast moves.

Whether you're relocating, buying a vehicle out of state, moving dealer inventory, or protecting a specialty car with enclosed service, the goal stays the same: keep transport straightforward and predictable.
